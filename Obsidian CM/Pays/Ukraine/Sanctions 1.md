Le concessionnaire de Bentley à Kiev s'est classé 3e au classement européen des meilleurs résultats de la marque en 2025. Voilà.

Yes, it's true. Bentley’s Europe regional director Richard Leopold announced it at the brand’s “Best of the Best by KPI” retailer awards in Marbella this month. Kyiv’s dealer took 3rd place for 2025 European performance (behind Padova, Italy #1 and Rotterdam #2). Average Bentley ~$400k. Confirmed in event coverage; the photo in the original post is from the ceremony.
